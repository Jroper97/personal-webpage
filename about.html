About Jewel Roper

My name is Jewel Roper. I am 25 years old. I am married, a mother of three; two boys and a girl. I currently work at Magna Mirrors in Newaygo as an assembler, assembling car mirrors and handles. I have a passion for doing puzzles and riddles.

I originally wanted to be a nurse. Once covid hit it delayed my schooling and got to the point where I realized that it wasn't what I wanted. I recently got into coding. To me it is like a puzzle. You think of what you want to do and how you want to display it. Then you figure out how to put it into code and boom. So far I find it challenging and fun.
